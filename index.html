Happy Mother's Day

To the woman who makes life beautiful

Dear Mother,

Thank you for your endless love, quiet sacrifices, and the warmth you bring into every room. You've been my guide, my comfort, and my greatest inspiration. You taught me how to bloom without ever demanding the sun. You loved me in the spaces between words, in the patience of your waiting, in the grace of your letting go. May the years ahead pour back into your hands abundant blessings. Today, I celebrate you not just as my mother, but as the incredible woman you are.

✦

"Because of you, I have a mother in this lifetime.
Because of you, I have a friend forever."

With all my love,
Your Daughter Michell 💖
